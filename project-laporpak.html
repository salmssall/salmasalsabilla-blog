LaporPak.com

An Innovative Public Reporting Platform

LaporPak.com is a digital platform designed to facilitate public issue reporting and monitor its follow-up process. This project was developed during the Second Hackathon, focusing on efficient reporting processes, and informative data visualization.

Key Contributions and Process

User Experience Testing
Efficient User Flow
Data Visualization Dashboard
Wireframes & Mockup

Final Outcome

LaporPak.com offers a digital solution that simplifies public issue reporting and allows users to efficiently track their report status. Its user-friendly interface and gamification features make the platform both effective and engaging.
