AgroTour

Digital Solution for Promoting Agro Tourism

AgroTour is a platform designed to enhance agro-tourism by providing users easy access to village tourism information. This project was developed during a National Hackathon, where our team worked to deliver an intuitive and user-friendly design solution based on a pre-defined case study.

Key Contributions and Process

Understanding User Needs
User Journey Mapping
Wireframing & Prototyping
Iterative Design Process

Final Outcome

AgroTour simplifies navigation through tourism destinations with an intuitive interface. The platform aims to boost local economic growth by connecting users with unique agrotourism experiences.
